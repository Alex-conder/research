Cross-comparison: docking energies and geometries
============================================================

Docking energies (kcal/mol):
  Meropenem apo:             -6.788
  Meropenem in Vancomycin-first: -6.738
  Vancomycin apo:            -8.743
  Vancomycin in Meropenem-first: -8.502

Docked ternary complex geometries:
  Meropenem-first VAN-MER distance: 7.38 A
  Vancomycin-first VAN-MER distance: 51.05 A

MM-GBSA binding free energies (igb=2, saltcon=0.15 M):
  Meropenem-first (Vancomycin as ligand):  -52.8460 +/- 4.2025 kcal/mol
  Vancomycin-first (Meropenem as ligand):  -23.0253 +/- 2.4839 kcal/mol
  Delta Delta G (Mero-first - Van-first):  -29.82 kcal/mol

Interpretation:
  - Meropenem-first: Vancomycin binds close to Meropenem, forming a crowded ternary complex.
  - Vancomycin-first: Meropenem binds far from Vancomycin, no ternary crowding; affinity is essentially apo-like.
  - Docking affinities alone suggest little difference, but geometry + MM-GBSA together reveal order-dependent paving.
